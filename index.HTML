Taco Trivia

Did you know that the first recorded mention of tacos dates back to the 18th century in the silver mines of Mexico? Miners would wrap gunpowder in paper and place it in a tortilla to create a makeshift explosion - hence the origin of the word "taco" meaning "wadding" or "plug" in Spanish.
